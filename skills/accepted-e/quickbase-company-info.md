## What Quickbase Does

Quickbase is the first application platform built for dynamic work. It makes it simple to bring together all data and teams into one centralised place — a no-code/low-code platform that lets business teams build custom apps, automate workflows, and manage complex operations without needing developers.

**Core value proposition:** When work has outgrown the spreadsheets, systems, and handoffs holding it together, Quickbase lets teams build connected apps that automate what's manual, see what's happening, and adapt as things change.
